## Carousel 

Carousel is based on [react-slick](https://github.com/akiran/react-slick) library 

For configuration and examples look at the [Documentation](https://react-slick.neostack.com/docs/get-started)


```jsx
        <Carousel>
          <div>
            <h3>1</h3>
          </div>
          <div>
            <h3>2</h3>
          </div>
          <div>
            <h3>3</h3>
          </div>
          <div>
            <h3>4</h3>
          </div>
          <div>
            <h3>5</h3>
          </div>
          <div>
            <h3>6</h3>
          </div>
        </Carousel>
```
