### Variants

```jsx
<Alert>
    <Alert.Header>Hey, I am title of default Alert!</Alert.Header>
    <Alert.Content>This is just example of content.</Alert.Content>
</Alert>
<br/>
<Alert variant="info">
    <Alert.Header>Hey, I am title of info Alert!</Alert.Header>
    <Alert.Content>This is just example of content.</Alert.Content>
</Alert>
<br/>
<Alert variant="danger">
    <Alert.Header>Hey, I am title of danger Alert!</Alert.Header>
    <Alert.Content>This is just example of content.</Alert.Content>
</Alert>
```
